What is Considered “Healthy Food”?

"Healthy Food” Defined

Healthy foods are those that provide you with the nutrients you need to sustain your body’s well-being and retain energy. Water, carbohydrates, fat, protein, vitamins, and minerals are the key nutrients that make up a healthy, balanced diet.This information tells us we need to focus on eating fresh foods that provide us with the nutrients our bodies need.
